Academic Reading Passage

The phenomenon of climate change has become increasingly apparent in recent decades. Scientists have been analyzing various indicators to understand the magnitude of environmental changes occurring worldwide.

Research has shown that the accumulation of greenhouse gases in the atmosphere is the primary catalyst for global warming. This process has accelerated due to human activities such as deforestation and industrial emissions.

The consequences of climate change are multifaceted and unprecedented. They include rising sea levels, extreme weather patterns, and the disruption of ecosystems around the globe.
